About us

Food
and Ambience

Le Bistro Central is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading Portugal restaurant group, Benoit Gardinier, and Executive Chef Gordon Ramsay.

The Bistro is an organic space reflective of Gordon Ramsay nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of Coimbra. An ode to the Portuguese landscape, from the vast ocean floor, to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.
